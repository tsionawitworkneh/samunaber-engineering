Our Story

Who We Are

Samunaber Engineering is a growing electro-mechanical and industrial engineering company based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Founded in 2012 by Asmamaw Tsegazeab as a small metal workshop in Yeka Sub-city, the company has evolved into a competitive and trusted engineering firm.

Built on a foundation of innovation, integrity, and technical excellence, Samunaber Engineering delivers high-quality solutions across structural, mechanical, and industrial projects. From design to execution, the team ensures precision, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

With a commitment to supporting Ethiopia's industrial growth, Samunaber Engineering continues to position itself as a trusted partner in delivering modern and sustainable engineering solutions.
